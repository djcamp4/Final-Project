Senior Backend Engineer
Location: Boston, MA (Hybrid — 3 days onsite)
Department: Engineering
Reports to: VP of Engineering

---

ABOUT THE ROLE

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our core platform team. You will design,
build, and scale the backend systems that power our SaaS product, which serves thousands of
enterprise customers and processes hundreds of millions of API requests per month. You will
work closely with product, frontend engineering, and infrastructure teams to deliver reliable,
high-performance features and lead technical improvements across the codebase.

---

REQUIRED SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS

- 5+ years of professional backend software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python — used daily in a production environment
- Deep experience with Django or Django REST Framework
- Expert-level PostgreSQL skills including query optimization, indexing, and schema design
- Experience designing and maintaining RESTful APIs at scale
- Proven ability to debug and improve performance in high-traffic production systems
- Experience with asynchronous task processing (Celery, Redis, or equivalent)
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure, preferably AWS (EC2, RDS, S3)
- Comfortable writing automated tests and working in a CI/CD environment
- Experience mentoring junior engineers or leading small technical teams

---

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

- Experience with Docker and container orchestration (Kubernetes a plus)
- Familiarity with FastAPI or other modern Python web frameworks
- AWS certification (Developer Associate or higher)
- Experience migrating or modernizing legacy systems
- Contributions to open source Python projects

---

RESPONSIBILITIES

- Design, build, and maintain scalable backend services and APIs consumed by web and
  mobile clients
- Own the reliability and performance of core platform services — set uptime targets,
  monitor systems, and lead incident response
- Identify and resolve database performance bottlenecks through query optimization,
  indexing strategies, and caching
- Collaborate with frontend engineers and product managers to design APIs and translate
  product requirements into technical solutions
- Write clean, well-tested, well-documented code and participate actively in code reviews
- Mentor junior and mid-level engineers through code reviews, pair programming, and
  design discussions
- Contribute to architectural decisions and help evolve the platform from its current
  state toward a more scalable, maintainable design

---

ABOUT US

We are a B2B SaaS company building data infrastructure tools for enterprise clients. Our
engineering team is small, collaborative, and moves fast. We value engineers who take
ownership, communicate clearly, and care about the quality of what they ship.

Compensation: $155,000 – $185,000 base + equity + benefits
